Retrieves data about a single link if the requesting advertiser/publisher has access to it. Advertisers can look up their own links. Publishers can look up visible links or links belonging to advertisers they are in a relationship with. (links.get)

Аргументи
string$roleThe role of the requester. Valid values: 'advertisers' or 'publishers'.
string$roleIdThe ID of the requesting advertiser or publisher.
string$linkIdThe ID of the link to look up.
